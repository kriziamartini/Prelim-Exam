About Aespa

Aespa's lore is based on the concept that we exist in two worlds: the Real World and the Virtual World. In the Virtual World we all have our own avatar, æ, that is created based on the data we have uploaded on the internet. They are the ones we choose to show to the world, the perfect online self. But the Virtual World isn't just the perfect happy place we think it is. In the darkness lives a virus called Black Mamba that disturbs the connection 'SYNK' between aespa and æs. Because of that, aespa went to the Virtual World KWANGYA to defeat Black Mamba with the help of nævis from FLAT to get rid of this virus. aespa successfully beat the virus using their superpowers and went back to the Real World but that caused strange things to happen in the Real World, a Strange Phenomenon.
